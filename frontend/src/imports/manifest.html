01 · Volume

DMK's manifesto is twice as long as ADMK's and 23× TVK's.

In pure count terms DMK committed to 524 numbered points, ADMK to 297, TVK to 23. TVK's document trades volume for brevity — an average of 59 words per point versus 32–35 for the major parties.

02 · Sector mix

Welfare dominates. Infrastructure follows.

DMK leans hard on education and infrastructure alongside welfare. ADMK's mix is weighted to agriculture and direct welfare transfers. TVK, far shorter, concentrates on governance and economic reform.

03 · Beneficiaries

Who gets named

Youth appear in 85 DMK promises — three times ADMK's count. Farmers are mentioned near-equally. SC/ST, differently abled and transgender beneficiaries are rare in all three.

Keyword match on English translations; does not account for implicit beneficiaries.
04 · Geography

Where the promises land

Both major parties cluster investments in Chennai and Coimbatore. Coastal districts feature heavily in ADMK's manifesto — unsurprising given the fishermen focus. Rural references outnumber urban in ADMK; DMK is the reverse.
